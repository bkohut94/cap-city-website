Services

Five core services for local Ottawa businesses. Each one focused on outcomes, not overhead.

Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

Most people find local businesses through Google. If your business isn't showing up for relevant searches in Ottawa, you're losing customers to competitors who are.

We focus on local SEO — the kind that gets you found by people who are nearby and ready to buy. We don't chase vanity rankings or promise page-one results overnight. We do the foundational work that builds long-term visibility.

What's included

  • Local keyword research focused on your service area
  • On-page optimization: titles, meta descriptions, headings, and content
  • Google Business Profile setup and ongoing optimization
  • Local directory and citation listings
  • Basic technical SEO audit and fixes
  • Monthly performance reporting

Web Development

Your website is often the first thing a potential customer sees. It needs to load fast, look professional on mobile, and make it easy for people to contact you or understand what you offer.

We build clean, functional websites for local businesses — no unnecessary complexity, no bloated page builders. Built to convert, not to impress web designers.

What's included

  • Custom design tailored to your business and industry
  • Mobile-first, fully responsive layout
  • Contact forms, maps, hours, and service pages
  • Fast load times and basic SEO structure built in
  • Hosting setup and ongoing maintenance
  • Simple content updates as needed

Social Media & Online Marketing

Consistency matters more than virality. A local business that posts professional, relevant content twice a week builds credibility and stays top of mind with customers — without needing a content strategy department.

We handle the posting so you don't have to think about it. Content is professional, on-brand, and appropriate for your audience. Nothing generic, nothing embarrassing.

What's included

  • 2 posts per week across your active platforms
  • Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn supported
  • Profile setup and optimization
  • Content creation appropriate to your industry
  • Monthly reach and engagement summary

Google Ads

Google Search ads put your business at the top of results when someone in Ottawa is actively searching for your service. Done properly, they're one of the highest-intent ways to reach new customers.

We set up and manage Search campaigns focused on buying-intent keywords. No display networks, no awareness campaigns — just ads that reach people who are ready to hire someone.

What's included

  • Campaign setup and keyword research
  • Ad copywriting and A/B testing
  • Bidding strategy and budget management
  • Negative keyword filtering to reduce wasted spend
  • Conversion tracking setup
  • Monthly performance review and optimization

Meta Ads (Facebook & Instagram)

Facebook and Instagram ads let you reach Ottawa residents by location, demographic, and interest — before they're even actively searching. They're particularly effective for local service businesses that want to build awareness and generate leads.

We manage campaigns from setup through to ongoing optimization. Ad creative and copy are included — you don't need to produce anything on your end.

What's included

  • Campaign setup and audience targeting
  • Ad creative and copywriting
  • Local geographic and demographic targeting
  • Budget management and bid optimization
  • Lead form or website traffic campaigns
  • Monthly results reporting
