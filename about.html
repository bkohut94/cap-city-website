About Cap City Marketing

A small Ottawa agency with a clear focus: help local businesses get their digital presence right, without overcomplicating it.

Who we are

Ottawa locals who know the work.

Cap City Marketing has been operating in Ottawa since 2017. We started the agency to build real-world marketing skills and work directly with local business owners — and that's still exactly what we do.

We're a small, intentional operation. No junior account managers, no revolving team, no handoffs. When you work with us, you work with us directly.

What we do

Digital foundations for local business.

We handle the digital work that keeps a local business visible and credible online: SEO, clean websites, consistent social media, and starter ad campaigns on Google and Meta.

Everything we do is aimed at one goal — getting your business in front of Ottawa customers who are already looking for what you offer.

Who we work with

Built for Ottawa's local businesses.
